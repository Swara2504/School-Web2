Alumni

Investiture Ceremony 2018-19 “A leader is one who knows the way, goes the way and shows the way.” By John Maxwell Amanora School celebrated the most coveted ‘Investiture Ceremony’ with pomp and show on 30th June 2018 at the Emerald Hall, The Fern, Amanora Park Town. The young cabinet council was sworn in and invested with power to don their mantle of leadership and responsibility. The democratically elected Student Council was chosen through relentless campaigning and digitised E-Polling making the election process transparent. The programme began with the Principal accompanying the Chief guest Brigadier Rajeev Sethi (Deputy GOC, Sub Area Dakshin Maharashtra). The event began with invoking the presence of the almighty with ‘Lamp Lighting’ followed by a musical rendition on ‘Leadership’, sung by the School Choir and perfect synergy displayed by the young leaders through the Slow March. The School Captain, Vice-Captain and other Cabinet members were sworn in and handed over their respective badges of responsibility by the Principal and the Chief Guest. Principal, Ms. Meera Nair administered the oath to the newly appointed School Cabinet who pledged to dispense their duties with commitment, hard work and determination. The swearing in ceremony was followed by a powerful acceptance speech delivered the School Captain Ms. Shreya Sandurkar, who spoke like a true leader and quoted Martin Luther King in nurturing a ‘vision’ and ‘dream’. A lively music recital was put up by the student choir in order to enliven the audience. The Chief Guest Brigadier Sethi congratulated the school for putting up an impeccable ceremony. In his address, he reminded the student leaders that with position comes responsibility towards themselves, their school and peers. He urged the students to become architects of their own future. The ceremony concluded on a note of rejoice as the students of Grade X 2017-18 batch were felicitated for their stellar performance in CBSE board examination. The event was anchored successfully by Ms. Debarati Ray and Coordinated by Grade IX and X Coordinator - Ms. Amrita Gangwal, Vice Principal - Ms. Madhulika Sharma proposed the vote of thanks to all the members of the team for their camaraderie in making the event a grand success.

Vidya Balan (pronounced [ʋɪd̪jaː baːlən]; born 1 January 1979) is an Indian actress. Shah ruk Khan

Shah ruk Khan

VShah Rukh Khan (pronounced [‘ʃaːɦrəx ˈxaːn]; born 2 November 1965), also known by the initialism SRK, is an Indian actor, film producer, and television personality. Referred to in the media as the "Baadshah of Bollywood" (in reference to his 1999 film Baadshah), "King of Bollywood" and "King Khan", he has appeared in more than 80 Hindi films, and earned numerous accolades, including 14 Filmfare Awards. The Government of India has awarded him the Padma Shri, and the Government of France has awarded him the Ordre des Arts et des Lettres and the Legion of Honour. Khan has a significant following in Asia and the Indian diaspora worldwide. In terms of audience size and income, he has been described as one of the most successful film stars in the world.
